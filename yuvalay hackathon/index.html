About

We believe that Hackers have power to change the scenario related to the issues we are facing right now. But to make this happen, we need to stand together and collaborate! Our mission is not just to build a mere platform but a vibrant community where the best talents are recognized by the leading industry partners and collaborate towards the future Innovation!! Are you Innovative and Creative in solving day-to-day challenges? Want to make dent in the society by doing something unusual? Are you a maker and can you hack something creatively? Is innovation your forte? Are you a tech-enthusiast, coding geek or even other than these 2 fields but intereste in Electronics? Lastly, are you a passionate about the technology?? If YES is your answer to even few questions stated above then we invite you to CREATIVITY Un-LTD. - one of the finest Hardware Hackathons of its kind to celebrate ‘The International Arduino Day’ at Vadodara. A student held, 24 hours Hackathon is inviting best 10 teams, a legion of 30 Enthusiastic Hackers under one roof to meet, create and bring to life an idea in a most creative way. Our focus is on letting your imagination flow free and enable you to push the boundaries of your own capabilities in under 24 Hours. We believe that best learning from the hackathon is the process of looking-at and solving a problem in a unique way and the whole process around it, rather than merely an outcome!

Who can participate?

This hackathon is free and open for all. If you are under 18 years of age, we will need a consent from your parents. For female participants, they need not have to stay at night for the hackathon!

Last date of registration?

We will be closing the registrations on 10th May, 2018. Selected teams will be declared by 11th May.

How big a team can be?

Maximum of 3 members. Tip: You can have team of people from different fields, as this hackathon not only focuses on Technical Side, but creativity will be mattered most!

How about individual entries?

This is a team event. We believe that you are stronger in a team than individual. So, lone rangers are not allowed. If at all you can’t find a team, we can help you to connect with others who have applied individually, but chances in getting selected as a lone applicant is less than in a team
